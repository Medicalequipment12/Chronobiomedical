Wheelchair Service and Maintenance

Reliable mobility solutions are essential for patient care. Chrono Biomedical offers complete service and maintenance for both manual and powered wheelchairs. We ensure safety, comfort, and mobility are never compromised.

From brake adjustments to motor diagnostics and battery checks, our trained technicians inspect, repair, and upgrade all wheelchair types to meet your operational needs.

Our Wheelchair Services Cover:

  • Manual and power wheelchair inspections
  • Battery testing and replacement
  • Tire and brake adjustments
  • Motor repair and controller diagnostics
  • Seating adjustments and alignment

Trust Chrono Biomedical to keep your mobility equipment safe, functional, and compliant with all medical standards.
