Wheelchair Service and Maintenance

Reliable mobility solutions are critical for patient care, and at Chrono Biomedical, we provide comprehensive service and maintenance for both manual and powered wheelchairs. Our goal is to ensure that safety, comfort, and mobility are always prioritized.

From brake adjustments to motor diagnostics and battery checks, our skilled technicians thoroughly inspect, repair, and upgrade all wheelchair types to meet your facility's operational needs.

Our Wheelchair Services Include:

  • Detailed inspections for manual and powered wheelchairs
  • Battery testing, replacement, and maintenance
  • Tire and brake adjustments for optimal performance
  • Motor repairs and controller diagnostics
  • Precision seating adjustments and alignment

Trust Chrono Biomedical to keep your mobility equipment safe, fully functional, and compliant with all medical standards. We help ensure your wheelchairs provide reliable support for patient care when it's needed most.
