Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

At Chrono Biomedical, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle any personal information you provide. This Privacy Policy describes the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Form Data: When you fill out our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Optional Metadata: Your IP address and browser metadata may be temporarily stored in our server logs for security and diagnostic purposes. This data is not linked to personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries or support requests promptly and effectively.
  • To improve our services and website usability based on user feedback and behavior (non-personally identifiable).
  • To maintain internal records and ensure reliable communication.

3. No Cookies or Analytics

We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools on our website at this time. Your browsing activity is not tracked for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data will only be disclosed if required by law, legal proceedings, or to protect the safety and rights of our company or users.

5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical measures to secure the data you submit through our contact form. Email communications are encrypted in transit, and access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. To make a request, contact us via email at info@chronobiomedical.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page.
