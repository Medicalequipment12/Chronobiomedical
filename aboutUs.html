About Chrono Biomedical

Chrono Biomedical was founded by seasoned veterans with over 20 years of experience servicing critical medical equipment in top-tier hospitals and healthcare institutions.

With deep industry insight and technical expertise, they established the company to provide specialized maintenance and repair services for essential equipment such as radiation oncology linear accelerators, suction regulators, and wheelchairs.

Today, Chrono Biomedical is known for its fast response times, certified engineering, and unwavering commitment to keeping healthcare operations running safely and efficiently.
